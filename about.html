Sophia Herradora Combes is a digital media artist at San Jose State University. From the moment they learned how to pick up a crayon, art has always been a core ingredient to who they are as a person. Most of their work consists of character illustrations and 3D work in MAYA, as well as logo and website design done in Adobe Illustrator. With an interest in illustration and game design, their goal is to pursue a higher education in art and share their artistic ideas with the world.
