*Important to mention: Although glitch art isn’t something I plan to pursue, it is a technique I’ve experimented with.

For these pieces, I used Notepad++ to manipulate artwork that I created in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The final image at the bottom is a photo of beans that I pulled from the internet and edited. Sadly I was unable to locate the original source. For all of these, I opened them in Notepad++ and manually modified the text. I experimented with adding and deleting text within the editor, often going back and forth to make sure that the image didn’t break. I found that the best method for this is to save after every change and open the image to see if I wanted to keep or discard the changes I made. I repeated this process until I had the desired outcome.
